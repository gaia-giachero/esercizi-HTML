BAKED TROFIE WITH SPECK AND WALNUTS

Ingredients Procedure Sources

Ingredients:

☞ For the pasta:

  • 500g fresh trofie
  • 200g speck
  • 90g walnuts
  • to taste rosemary
  • 30g grated parmesan cheese
  • 180g provola
  • to taste extra virgin olive oil
Main Ingredients

☞ For the béchamel sauce:

  • 700g milk
  • 70g butter
  • 70g pastry flour (farina 00)
  • to taste salt and pepper

Procedure:

  1. Mince the speck, walnuts, and rosemary and sauté in a pan with salt and pepper.
  2. Prepare the béchamel sauce:
    • combine the flour and butter, place over heat and let melt over low heat;
    • add the hot milk, stir and let cook (the consistency should not be too thick).
  3. Cook the pasta for a few minutes and drain it.
  4. Combine the pasta, a portion of the béchamel, and provola.
  5. Mix, place in a baking dish, add the remaining béchamel, the provola, and the grated parmesan.
  6. Bake in the oven at 200°C for 20 minutes.
